# NNPC — Time Series Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project analyzes weekly petroleum sales data from the Nigerian 
National Petroleum Corporation (NNPC) across three years from 2022 
to 2024 to identify trends, seasonal patterns and performance changes.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Plotly
- Dash

## Dataset
576 rows of weekly data covering four petroleum products — Premium 
Motor Spirit (PMS), Automotive Gas Oil (AGO), Dual Purpose Kerosene 
(DPK) and Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG).

## Dashboard Features
- KPI cards showing Total Revenue, Total Volume, Avg Price per Litre and Max Stations
- Total Revenue Trend line chart
- Volume Sold by Product horizontal bar chart
- Revenue Share by Product donut chart
- Price per Litre Trend by Product line chart
- Volume by Year and Product grouped bar chart
- Avg Stations Supplied area chart
